How TO MAKE A PERFECT MAGGIE

Maggie is a delicious cuisine which is widely eaten in India. It can be be bought at just a price of 14rs. It is made from both atta and maida. It is made from a boiling half cup of water and a packet of maggie noodles. Start by adding the noodles to boiling water, then add tastemaker and stir constantly. Add more water if you are a soupy maggie lover. After 3 minutes of stirring, taste a noodle of maggie by using a fork, if it is soft then your maggie is ready, if not then cook more. after softening of maggie serve in a plate HOT and enjoy your maggie with a glass of coke.
